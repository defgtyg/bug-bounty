# Checkpoints In The Review

A brief arrives with edges clean,
its scope held still in morning light;
we read the surface, then between
small promises the tests must write.

The endpoint hums behind the page,
a ledger waits for proof to land;
each claim is narrowed, line and stage,
until the risk is named by hand.

A patch walks slowly through the gate,
with failing paths made plain and true;
maintainers weigh the changed state,
and ship the fix the users knew.

When payout follows merged intent,
the record keeps a quieter grace:
secure work done, review well spent,
and trust restored to its place.
